About XSalary

A study of nearly 2800 students in years 10 to 12 showed one-third felt they had insufficient information to make career choices. There is an invisible gap between teenagers and information about future careers. A lot of school students are falling behind due to not getting the careers support they need.
Our goal is to eliminate that gap and reduce inequality by focusing on analyzing salary, and possibly combining individual personality tests. Our website combines salary analysis and personality testing to provide you with personalized career selection recommendations.
